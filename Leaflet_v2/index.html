Instructions

  1. Zoom in and out using your mouse or the zoom in and out buttons on the top left of the map
  2. To change map, click the white box in the top right corner of the map
  3. Drag your mouse across the map or use arrow keys in the direction you want
  4. Click the badges on the map to see the name of the football club
  5. If you are unable to use the map then please click on 'clubs' link in the navigation bar at the top of the page
